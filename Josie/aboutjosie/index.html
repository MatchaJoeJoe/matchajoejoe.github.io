Empowering girls to pursue careers in science

Josie Robin, future PhD, wants nothing more than to become a scientist when she grows up. She’s a girl who, when faced with a problem, knows there’s a scientific solution out there…and stops at nothing to find it.

Josie Robin’s incredible and fun adventures bring the world of science to vivid and dramatic life. In a series of stories, Josie Robin will:

  • Shrink down to a microscopic size to fight the flu bug in her little brother’s body!
  • Reach Earth’s core using only a plastic shovel!
  • Go up to the sky and reason with angry clouds!
  • Join a hard-at-work colony of ants on an important mission!
  • And much, much more!
