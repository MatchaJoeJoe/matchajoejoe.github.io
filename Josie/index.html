All around us, there are fascinating things happening that we can’t even see. Plants are having to-the-death territorial battles with other plants! The sun’s magnetic field is changing direction! Microscopic organisms are at war…inside our bodies!

Science is happening. And it’s amazing!

What if there was a place where all this science came to vivid and dramatic life…and we could see it? Well, this place exists! In the world of JOSIE ROBIN, SCIENCE FIEND.

JOSIE ROBIN, SCIENCE FIEND is an adventure series with heart and humor that leads kids on mind-expanding tours through larger-than-life science-scapes.

Stay tuned for the book that inspired the series... A FUNGUS AMONG US!

In Josie's first adventure, she faces off with a pesky organism that's taking over her world and proves she has not only the moxie to take charge of difficult situations, but also the smarts to resolve them!
