So I got this idea at TOC 2013. It was inspired by the Git workshop and the Medium Keynote.

The idea is a pretty simple one. I wrote a story, turned it into a website, and now I want to make it better. The problem is, I personally do not have the time or resources to make better, at least not as good as it could be.

That's where you come in. I'm opening my book up to the public to see how good it can be.

I've posted the html code on GitHub here.

Any suggestions are welcome. I will be making updates to the site as often as possible, hopefully at least once a week.

If you would like to see how the book is progressing, you can go here.

Just to clarify, I don't plan on trying to make any money from this project, I just want to see what would happen if I put my idea out there and let it grow.
